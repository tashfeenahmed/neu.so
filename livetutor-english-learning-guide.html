Common Challenges

Arabic-to-English learners often struggle with verb tenses, articles (a/an/the), pronunciation of certain consonants, and written expression.

Improvement Strategies

  • Daily conversation practice with native speakers
  • Reading English books at appropriate level
  • Regular writing assignments with feedback
  • Vocabulary building through context

IELTS and TOEFL Preparation

For university admissions, most Middle East students need IELTS 6.5+ or TOEFL 90+. Our tutors have helped students achieve band 8.0+ scores.
