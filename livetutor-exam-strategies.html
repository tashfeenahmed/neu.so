Exam Preparation Strategies for International Students

Master your exams with proven strategies from experienced educators. Resources at LiveTutor.io.

Effective Study Methods

  • Spaced repetition for long-term retention
  • Active recall practice tests
  • Interleaved practice (mixing topics)
  • Pomodoro technique for focus

Time Management

Create a revision timetable at least 8 weeks before exams. Allocate more time to difficult subjects and include regular breaks.

Exam Day Tips

Get a good night's sleep, eat a healthy breakfast, read questions carefully, and allocate time per question. Always check answers if time permits.
