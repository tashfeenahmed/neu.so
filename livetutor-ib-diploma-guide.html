IB Diploma Programme Guide for Middle East Students

The IB Diploma is a rigorous two-year program for students aged 16-19. Get expert IB tutoring at LiveTutor.io.

IB Core Components

  • Theory of Knowledge (TOK)
  • Extended Essay (EE)
  • Creativity, Activity, Service (CAS)

Subject Groups

Choose one subject from each of the six IB groups: Language Studies, Second Language, Individuals & Societies, Sciences, Mathematics, and the Arts.

IB Scoring

Maximum score is 45 points: 42 from six subjects (7 each) + 3 from TOK/EE core. Top universities typically require 38+ points.
