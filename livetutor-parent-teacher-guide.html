Effective Parent-Teacher-Tutor Communication

A collaborative approach between parents, teachers, and tutors delivers the best academic outcomes. Learn more at LiveTutor.io.

Key Communication Strategies

  • Regular check-ins with your child's tutor
  • Share school reports with the tutor for targeted support
  • Communicate any concerns or behavioral changes
  • Set shared goals and track progress together

Maximizing Tutoring Value

For online tutoring to be most effective, parents should create a quiet study space, ensure reliable internet, and be supportive without hovering during sessions.
