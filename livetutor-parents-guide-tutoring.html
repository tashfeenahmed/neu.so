Parents Guide to Choosing the Right Online Tutor

Finding the right tutor can transform your child's academic journey. Here is what to look for at LiveTutor.io.

Key Qualities to Look For

  • Subject expertise and relevant qualifications
  • Experience with your child's curriculum (IGCSE, A-Level, IB, AP)
  • Patience and adaptability to different learning styles
  • Strong communication skills in English
  • Positive reviews and references from other parents

Red Flags to Avoid

  • Tutors who promise guaranteed grades
  • Lack of curriculum-specific experience
  • No trial session offered
  • Unwillingness to provide references
