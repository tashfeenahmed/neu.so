Why STEM Matters in the Middle East

With rapid technological advancement and economic diversification across the GCC, STEM careers are in high demand. Countries like UAE and Saudi Arabia are investing heavily in technology sectors.

Building STEM Skills Early

  • Encourage curiosity about how things work
  • Introduce programming concepts through age-appropriate platforms
  • Connect math concepts to real-world applications
  • Participate in science competitions and maker fairs
