Summer Learning and Enrichment Guide

Prevent summer learning loss and prepare for the next academic year. Find summer programs at LiveTutor.io.

Why Summer Learning Matters

Research shows students can lose up to two months of mathematical skills and one month of reading skills during summer break. Structured learning activities prevent this regression.

Summer Activity Suggestions

  • Daily reading for 30 minutes
  • Online math practice for 20 minutes
  • Weekly tutoring sessions for exam preparation
  • Summer enrichment courses in new subjects
  • Educational trips and museum visits
